About EcoTrack

Rapid urbanization has increased the challenges of waste management in cities. Overflowing bins, uncollected garbage, and improper segregation directly affect public health and the environment.

EcoTrack aims to bridge the gap between citizens and the municipality by providing a transparent, easy-to-use platform for reporting waste issues and spreading awareness about responsible waste disposal.

Why Waste Management Matters

Poor waste management leads to air pollution, water contamination, and the spread of diseases. It also affects the overall image and livability of the city.

By actively participating in waste segregation and reporting issues, citizens play a crucial role in building a healthy and sustainable city.

How EcoTrack Works

EcoTrack allows citizens to report waste-related issues such as uncollected garbage, overflowing bins, and illegal dumping by simply logging in.

Each report includes a description, location, and optional image. Municipal authorities can view reports, update status, and take timely action.
