About EcoTrack

EcoTrack is a community-driven initiative to promote awareness about sustainable waste management. The project aims to bridge the gap between citizens and local authorities by providing a digital platform for reporting waste issues and spreading knowledge about recycling, composting, and smart city initiatives.

🌍 Our Mission

To create a cleaner, greener, and smarter city by combining technology, awareness, and citizen participation.

🏙️ Smart Waste Management System

In smart cities like Ahmednagar, waste management departments use digital dashboards, IoT-enabled sensors, and GPS-tracked collection vehicles to monitor and optimize the collection process. By integrating data and awareness, cities aim to reduce landfill waste and improve recycling efficiency.

♻️ Waste Segregation Guide

Dry Waste

Paper, plastic, glass, and metal. Keep them clean and dry for recycling.

Wet Waste

Kitchen waste, vegetable peels, and garden waste. Ideal for composting.

Hazardous Waste

Batteries, e-waste, chemicals, and paints. Dispose at authorized centers.

💬 Citizen’s Role

Every citizen plays a key role in maintaining cleanliness. By segregating waste at home, participating in awareness drives, and reporting unattended waste, we can build a cleaner city together.
